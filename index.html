About Us

Our Mission

The Gilman Investment Society is dedicated to educating students about financial markets, investment strategies, and careers in finance.

What We Do

Through regular meetings and competitions, we provide hands-on experience in stock analysis, portfolio management, and market research. Our members learn from alumni and gain real-world investment experience either through competitions or the committee.

Join Us

Whether you're a beginner or have experience in investing, all students are welcome to join our community of learners and future financial leaders.
