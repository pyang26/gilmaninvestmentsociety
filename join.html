The Gilman Investment Committee

The Gilman Investment Committee is comprised of a dedicated group of students who pitch 3-4 stocks yearly to the rest of the committee and manage a $22k portfolio in school endowment.

The committee meets every Tuesday at 8:00 PM through Zoom meetings.

Entrance to the committee is by interview, which are hosted every year in the fall.
